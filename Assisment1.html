Information About Darjeeling

    Darjeeling – The Queen of the Hills Darjeeling, often referred to as the “Queen of the Hills,” is a picturesque hill station located in the state of West Bengal, India. Nestled in the Lesser Himalayas at an altitude of about 6,700 feet, Darjeeling is renowned for its scenic beauty, pleasant climate, rich cultural heritage, and world-famous tea. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in India, attracting visitors from all over the world.
    Natural Beauty and Landscape Darjeeling is blessed with stunning natural landscapes. The town offers panoramic views of snow-capped peaks, including the majestic Kanchenjunga, the third-highest mountain in the world. The lush green hills, dense forests, and terraced tea gardens contribute to its breathtaking beauty. The sunrise view from Tiger Hill is a mesmerizing experience, where the first rays of the sun light up the Himalayan peaks with golden hues.
